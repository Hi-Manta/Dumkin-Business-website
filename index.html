Our Story

Born on the Streets of Bhatiari

DUMKIN has become a beloved fast-food destination in Bhatiari, Chittagong — serving freshly prepared shawarma, burgers, momos, and grills at prices that make great food accessible to everyone. What started as one of the first food carts in the area has grown into a proper sit-down spot, without ever losing its street-food soul.
